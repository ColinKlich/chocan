    Design Analysis

    The proposed design exhibits a robust form of cohesion, which is intentionally achieved. This high level of cohesion arises from the strong interconnections between the actions performed by the various modules. However, it lacks substantial logical, temporal, or procedural cohesion, as these actions are not closely tied in a sequential or time-dependent manner. While there are instances of functional cohesion, its practicality is limited due to the complexity of the required modules. As a result, the predominant form of cohesion in this design is informational cohesion. This is evident in how most of our modules execute a range of actions, each having its distinct entry point and independent code. Though the design does show some degree of content coupling, it notably lacks common coupling or stamp coupling. Instead, the primary coupling method employed is data coupling, which is demonstrated by the modules sharing homogeneous data items, with all their elements being utilized by the modules they call.
